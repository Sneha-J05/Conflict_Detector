1
Each supervisory authority shall draw up an annual report on its activities, which may include a list of types of infringement notified and types of measures taken in accordance with 
Article 58
(2). 
2
Those reports shall be transmitted to the national parliament, the government and other authorities as designated by Member State law. 
3
They shall be made available to the public, to the Commission and to the Board.
